Day 8, the Little Sweet inside the hotel near Union Square. $6.50, whole milk,
unsweetened. This was actually the first hojicha I tried, before the series, and
now that I've tried enough to compare, it's my favorite so far. Not bitter, good
tea-roast flavor. I don't know why I like it this much, it's just good.
